About Rutvik Bhatt

Technologist & Product Manager

Rutvik Bhatt is a Technologist, Engineer turned product leader, and expert in product management practices who’s been hard at work for over 10 years. Having done product in multiple global markets, Rutvik has experience and insights that are rare to find in the global market. He’s helped shape the discipline of product management, built self-functional and self-sustainable team, helping product to grow in various domains. His work is focused on helping grow the next generation of product leadership, and helping bring a global perspective to product management practices everywhere.

Rutvik has a very unique blend of technical mindset and product leadership. His very data driven decision making process made sure that each product makes it mark on the global leaderboards. His work experience varies from a tech architectural background to leadership and strategy.

Rutvik started his career as a Software developer learning and developing multiple products for different global markets, satisfying different needs. His strong observational power helped him climbed the ladder up quickly to lead the team of application developers using architectural paradigms.

His unique skillset in technical expertise, and eagerness to learn more led him to manage the Full stack team of more than 28 Engineers working and developing solutions for North America, UK and Europe, APAC and US. He did not kill his eagerness to learn new technologies and architectural patterns as soon as he jumped into the people management, instead he kept learning and played active role at various stages of the tech, including Software architectures, Application communication protocols, cloud management, DevOps and Computing.

He realised very soon at work that developing applications are not enough, its also important to manage the business expectations and gained experience by managing development teams using Agile and kept experimenting various project management methodologies.

He understood the power of Cloud computing and race to develop faster and scalable applications to increase the user experience and meet the demand, he developed his interest in DevOps, managing highly scalable and efficient cloud architectures, plugged in with Continuous Integration with codebase and continuously delivering them to the user, with out a single human interaction.

All of these experiences moulded him to become a better product manager who actually understands what works and what not, by his own tried & tested methods. His great sense of understanding human behavioural patterns for applications and analytical, data-driven mindset helped him to ship habit building applications very quick and in a efficient manner.
